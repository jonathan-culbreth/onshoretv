Our Offices
Macon, MO
Onshore's rural delivery center located in Macon, Missouri. This 50,000 square foot facility is currently home to a broad spectrum of IT specialists and possesses a 1,200 square foot training facility. It is supported directly by our corporate headquarters, a 6,000 square foot facility with an additional 1,000 square foot training center. These facilities give Onshore the ability to react quickly to our customer needs.
Glennville, GA
Glennville is located in Tattnall County, Georgia and has a population of 3,363 and is located about an hour and fifteen minutes West of Savannah. The main building is the recently retrofitted city schoolhouse, originally built in 1915. This facility has the ability to quickly scale with our customer's needs.
