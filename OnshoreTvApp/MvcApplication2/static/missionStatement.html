Mission Statement

Onshore Outsourcing is an information technology and business services outsourcing firm. We are advancing America's ability to compete on a global scale by retooling our rural American workforce as information technology and business professionals. We apply transformational workforce development and service delivery techniques to provide low-cost, high-value business and technology services to leading firms across the nation. It is our awesome opportunity and privilege, to help our employees see beyond their current situation toward a better way of life...restoring hope, dignity, and the America dream throughout rural America.
